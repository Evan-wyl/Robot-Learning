## Requirement

- nvidia-driver-525-server
- Cuda 11.3
- PyTorch 1.10
- IsaacGym
- rsl_rl v1.0.2



## Usage

- `python legged_gym/script/train.py --task=anymal_c_flat --sim_device=cuda --rl_device=cuda --headless `

- `python legged_gym/script/train.py --task=anymal_c_flat --sim_device=cuda --rl_device=cuda  `
